A TRIP THROUGH MEMORY LANE ON PIZZA PALACE


THE PIZZA PALACE WAS FIRST FOUNDED AND ESTABLISHED IN 1999 BY MARK HOENHEIM.
HE WAS INSPIRED BY ALL THE DIFFERENT PIZZA'S HE TASTED WHILE TRAVELLING AROUND THE WORLD, HE WANTED TO BRING ALL THE DIFFERENT TYPES OF PIZZA AND FLAVOUR INTO ONE RESTAURANT.
WITH THE INGREDIENTS HE PICKED UP FROM ACROSS THE WORLD LIKE TOPPINGS FROM ITALY AND CHEESE FROM FRANCE HIS DREAM BEGAN TO COME INTO VIEW
AND THIS IS THE FINAL RESULT
